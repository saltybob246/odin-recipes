Olive Oil Bread

Description

A simple bread recipe, because the best breads are. This recipe is very versatile. If you would like to use it for Pizza Dough, or Dinner Bun dough, simply add 1/4 cup more water. If you find your dough to be too sticky, simple add a handful of flour to the dough at a time until the dough becomes workable.
