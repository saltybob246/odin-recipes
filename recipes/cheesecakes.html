Mini Cheese Cakes

Description

Fluffy creamy delicious little clouds. I like to top them with cherry pie filling, but they also taste great with any berries.

  • 1 12 ounce package of vanilla wafers
  • 2 8 ounce packages of cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 21 ounce can cherry pie filling

Directions

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F 175 degrees C. Line two 24 cup miniature muffin tins with paper liners
  3. Crush wafers, Press 1/2 teaspoon into each paper cup
  4. Beat cream cheese,sugar,eggs, and vanilla in a mixing bowl until light and fluffy. Fill each muffin liner 3/4 full with mixture.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 mins until the cheese cake is set. Top with teaspoon of cherry pie filling
  6. Serve and enjoy!
